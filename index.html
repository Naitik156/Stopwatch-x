How It Works

  1. Click "Start" to begin tracking your study session
  2. Keep your face visible to the camera
  3. The timer runs when you're focused (head down, looking at screen)
  4. Timer pauses automatically if you look away or leave the frame
  5. Click "Pause" to manually pause or "Reset" to start over

Privacy Note: All face processing happens in your browser. No images or video are stored or sent to any server.
